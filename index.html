Question-Based Studying

Test yourself with exam type questions and scenarios. Our content is created and continually updated by Canadian licensed pharmacists and past PEBC OSCE assessors. With over 800 MCQ questions and OSCE scenarios that cover all 9 competencies, our app provides a large sample and plenty of practice for the PEBC qualifying exams.

Keep It Simple

Easy and simple to use. Our MCQ section provides instant feedback for answers, along with your raw score. The OSCE section features a door prompt, followed by detailed cases for the standardized actor and the evaluation sheet. A timer is included to facilitate your practice sessions.

OSCE tip: Practice with a partner! Read the door prompt. Then pass the phone to your partner to start the timer and be your standardized patient.
