PEBC MCQ Questions and OSCE Cases

Test yourself with exam type questions and scenarios. Our content is created and continually updated by Canadian licensed pharmacists and past PEBC OSCE assessors. With over 850 MCQ in our question bank and OSCE cases that cover all 9 competencies, our app helps prepare you for the PEBC qualifying exams.

Questions are randomized every time you enter the module for unique practice sessions.

Useful Features

Easy and simple to use. Our MCQ section provides instant feedback for answers, along with your raw score. The app also keeps track of your cumulative scores broken down into categories. The OSCE section features a door prompt, followed by detailed cases for the standardized actor and the evaluation sheet. A timer is included to facilitate your practice sessions.

OSCE tip: Practice with a partner! Read the door prompt. Then pass the phone to your partner to start the timer and be your standardized patient.
